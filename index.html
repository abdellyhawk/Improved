what are gaming pcs?

A gaming computer, also known as a gaming PC, is a specialized personal computer designed for playing PC games at high standards. Gaming PCs typically differ from mainstream personal computers by using high-performance graphics cards, a high core-count CPU with raw performance and higher-performance RAM

But what about the cost?

The average gaming PC price will typically fall between $1,000 and $4,000 when considering a budget. However, a reasonable budget for a gaming PC can range between $1,000 and $2,500, although getting a PC below $1,000 and even $500 is possible, however, the quality might not be the best

But what should you get?

All it depends on is how much you are willing to spend and what games you are into. If you are someone who plays first person shooter, you obvosuily need something of high quaility to compete in such games. If you are more of a chill player and like to play things like story or open world games, high quailty isnt neccary and you can just get an average PC
